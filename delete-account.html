Delete Account

If you would like to delete your Finvest account, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Finvest app
  2. Go to the settings menu
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

What happens after deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your personal and financial data will be removed from our systems

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to access your account, you can request deletion by contacting us at:

contatowolfpackinnovation@gmail.com

Data retention

Some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
